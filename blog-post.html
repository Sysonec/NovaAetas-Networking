Top 10 wireless technologies

Fundations of modern networking are comprehensive and can be used in a broad range of industries across the globe. The most important are Software-defined networking (SDN) and network functions virtualization (NFV). These concepts are about to bring the "next-generation" networking infrastructure.

Artificial intelligence brings a bright future to the networking industry providing clear idea what is about to change within the industry. Many process will be easily automated due to the evolving smart behaviour of machines and servers.

Modern networking technology is rapidly evolving and getting more convenient, meaning wireless networking is getting even more developed in terms of conveniency! These are the newest wireless technologies that are at the top of the networking game.

Managing a modern network requires deep insights into how all its different pieces work in concert – and often rapid reactions to quickly-changing conditions that are unique to every network. In other words, understanding a network’s health takes pattern-recognition skills.
