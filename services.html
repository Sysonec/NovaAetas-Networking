Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need security tests?
Everyone who is active on any network needs periodical security tests to ensure their data safety.

How can I speed up my connection?
It's quite simple. Just update your hardware and let professional take care of your network.

Do I need to setup my firewall?
Firewall is crucial part of your network, it is a shield that protects you from threats that might come in and out of your network.

Is gathering network data helpful?
Certainly. For you to gather data in a proper way, you would need help from one of our engineers who can setup automation data tests. Additionally, it can tell you about condition of your home network.

Wireless or wired? Which is better?
There is no simple answer for that. This totally depends on environmental aspects upon our engineers will decide which type you should go with.

How can I take advantage of my network?
Network connection is vital for many parts of today's life. You can perform tasks related to your job or buy needed items with online payment. It's up to you to decide whether or not it is helpful.
